THE ROUTE

When collecting data, I had not planned a route to take; the intention was to wander around my bourough in the hunt for areas of nature. The images pinned to the map are of the route that I had taken along with a friend. Throughout our journey, we had stumbled across a gallery, an animal park, outdoor cafe’s and a Victorian cemetery.
